About the Data

Survey Design and Collection

This survey is a long running census of all of the stores, restaurants, bars, and other establishments in the city of Cleveland. It aims to collect information on the accessibility to a variety of products, including healthy foods, as well as drugs and other health products.

The information on these establishments is collected annually during the summer by a team of volunteers. The details are collected primarily via groundtruthing.

On account of the survey being long running, it contains a multitude of establishments that have since closed or changed property type. As of the summer of 2025, the data contains 699 closed establishments and 1639 currently open establishments.

Tobacco Sales

Establishments that sold tobacco products were counted and are displayed below. Tobacco products considered include the following:

  • Cigarettes

  • Menthol cigarettes

  • ENDS

  • Cigarillos/Little Cigars

  • Nicotine Pouches

  • Roll-Your-Own Tobacco

The following establishments are omitted from this list:

  • Restaurants (Full-service and Limited-service/Fast-food)

  • Bars and Liquor Stores

  • Closed Stores/Restaurants

  • Tobacco/Smoking Suppliers

    -> It is assumed, for this report, that all of these types of stores sold tobacco

Tobacco 21

Using this data, we are able to determine how many of the stores that do sell tobacco have proper local, state, or federal Tobacco 21 signage.

Below, we can see the number of establishments selling tobacco that did / did not have proper Tobacco 21 signage, organized by establishment type, as well as a proportion of those establishments that had proper signage.
